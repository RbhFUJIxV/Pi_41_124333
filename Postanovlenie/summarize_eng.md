# Summary of the Decree of the Government of the Russian Federation dated July 6, 2015 No. 676

## General Provisions
The decree approves requirements for the full lifecycle of state information systems (GIS): creation, development, commissioning, operation, decommissioning, and further storage of data. The requirements are mandatory for federal and regional executive authorities.

## Key Stages and Requirements

### 1. System Creation
*   **Basis:** A direct statutory mandate or an internal decision by the government body.
*   **Process:** Strictly according to an approved **technical specification**. Includes stages:
    *   Development of design and operational documentation.
    *   Development/adaptation of software.
    *   Startup and adjustment works.
    *   Preliminary tests.
    *   **Pilot operation** (identifying and eliminating deficiencies).
    *   **Acceptance tests.**

### 2. System Commissioning
*   The basis is the issuance of a **legal act** by the executive authority.
*   The act defines the list of preparatory measures and the **operation start date**.
*   Operation cannot begin before the completion of all measures mentioned in the act.

### 3. System Operation
*   Begins on the date specified in the commissioning act.
*   Must be carried out strictly in accordance with the approved **operational documentation**.

### 4. System Development
*   Modernization and development of the system are carried out according to the same rules as its **creation**.

### 5. System Decommissioning and Data Storage
*   **Grounds for decommissioning:** Expiration of service life, inexpediency, inefficiency, changes in legislation.
*   **Process:** A **legal act** is issued, which defines:
    *   Decommissioning activities (dismantling, disposal of equipment and software).
    *   **Procedure and storage periods for information.** Key requirement: data storage periods from the GIS **cannot be less than** the storage periods for similar paper documents.
    *   Procedure for informing users about the system's decommissioning.

## Important Notes
*   **Funding:** All activities are carried out within the allocated budget appropriations.
*   **Applicability:** The requirements are **recommendatory** for other state bodies (besides executive authorities), state corporations, extra-budgetary funds, and local self-government bodies.
